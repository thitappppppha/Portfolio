Project Goal

In today's fast-paced world, numerous applications offer convenience in areas like food delivery and transportation. Similarly, our application is designed to provide pet owners with an effortless shopping experience, empowering them to choose the best products for their pets. Our primary focus is to ensure that the app is intuitive and user-friendly, so users can enjoy the convenience of finding high-quality pet products without any difficulty.













Research and Ideation

According to a survey conducted by Rakuten Insight in January 2022, approximately 71% of Thai respondents reported owning at least one pet. This high level of pet ownership underscores the demand for specialized services tailored to pet owners. Our interest lies in developing an application that caters specifically to this growing market, offering a seamless online shopping experience exclusively for pet products, with convenient home delivery services directly to the customer's doorstep.







Lessons Learned

Designing this app has taught me a range of important lessons, especially in creating a more polished and professional user experience.

Asking question

I constantly question my design choices, evaluating why I made certain decisions—such as the placement of key buttons or the overall layout. This process allows me to refine each aspect, ensuring that clients find the application easy and intuitive to use.

Survey

I consistently research information on pet owners and have found that many people are dedicated to caring for their pets. I began asking about their specific needs and carefully noting these insights to develop a truly helpful application tailored to them.

Solve the problem

I frequently encounter challenges with my designs, such as instances where the user experience may be less intuitive for clients. These challenges motivate me to find solutions, and I find great satisfaction in resolving them.

User pain point

I have identified several pain points for my users, particularly their lack of time for shopping, including for pet products. I aim to create an experience that allows them to feel as though they have a pet shop at home, featuring a curated selection that combines both everyday and luxury pet brands.
